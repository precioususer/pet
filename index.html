# DESING ROOM

This is a creative space where every corner is filled with art and inspiration. Design Room gathers talented artists for whom painting is not just a hobby, but a true passion. Unique paintings are created here, reflecting a diversity of styles and techniques: from classical oil canvases to bold experiments with acrylic and mixed media. The approach to art is individual — there is a commitment to ensuring that each artwork carries a part of the artist's soul and interacts with the space and emotions of the viewer.
